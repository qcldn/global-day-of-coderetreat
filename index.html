What is Coderetreat?

Coderetreat is a really fun day with the goal to improve our coding skills and learn from each other.

There will be groups from all over the world participating.

It follows a particular structure:

  • Start with a simple programming problem: Conway's Game of Life
  • Work with different partners to discuss and solve the problem
  • Add restrictions to force you to consider different approaches
  • After each round, throw out your code, discuss your experience and repeat!

Frequently asked questions

Do I need to bring anything?

Yes! Please bring your laptop and have it set up with development environments for your favourite programming languages. We will be using test driven development, so make sure to hook up a test framework, too.

How much programming experience do I need?

You need to have some experience with at least one programming language. But it doesn't matter if you are still trying to understand the ins and outs of your first language or you have 20 years of experience in all sorts of languages - everyone will learn something new during this event.
